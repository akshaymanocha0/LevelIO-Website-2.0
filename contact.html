Common Questions

FAQs

Things people usually ask before their first build.

How long does a typical build take?

Simple setups (PC workstation or console lounge) typically take 1–3 days on-site. Full room builds and gaming cafe projects range from 1–3 weeks depending on scope and size. We'll give you an accurate timeline during your consultation.

Do you supply the hardware and furniture too?

Yes. We can handle end-to-end procurement of monitors, desks, chairs, peripherals, lighting, panels, and all tech components through our brand partnerships. Or we can work with hardware you've already purchased — your choice.

What cities do you operate in?

We currently operate across Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, Jaipur, Ahmedabad, and several other tier-2 cities. Don't see yours? Contact us — we travel for the right project.

Is the consultation really free?

100% free, no strings attached. We'll have a call, understand your space and budget, and put together an initial proposal. You decide whether to move forward. No hard selling, ever.

What does the warranty cover?

We provide a workmanship warranty on all our installation work — this covers cable management, mounting, panel fitting, and lighting installation. Hardware warranties are passed directly from the manufacturer to you.
